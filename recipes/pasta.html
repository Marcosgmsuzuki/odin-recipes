Pasta

Description

make this pasta and give it a change before the week starts!

A good Sunday pasta is unbeatable!

Igredients

  • 1 package of spaghetti pasta
  • 500g of minced meat
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 piece of onion
  • 2 tablespoons of green olives
  • 10 basil leaves
  • soy oil to taste
  • grated cheese to taste

Step by step

  1. In a pan, put a thread of oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic.
  2. Add the meat and sauté until it's well cooked and golden.
  3. Add the sauce, olives and season with salt. Let it cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Then place the leaves of basil and turn off the heat.
  5. finish with grated cheese and serve.
  6. enjoy the meal!
