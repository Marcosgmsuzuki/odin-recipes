Lasagna

Descrição

The family's favorite pasta in versions everyone will love

This democratic baked pasta with layers of sauce and cheese is something that is a hit even with the most demanding palates.

Igredients

  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 chopped medium onion (150g)
  • 400g ground beef
  • 2 diced medium tomatoes (300g)
  • 2 tomato pulp boxes (1kg)
  • half a cup of water (1ooml)
  • 2 sazon seasoning sachets
  • half a teaspoon of salt
  • 1 oven-dried lasagna noodles (250g)
  • 200g sliced ham
  • 300g sliced mozzarella

Step by step

  1. In a large pan, add the oil and lead to high fire to warm up. join onion and sauté for 2 minutes. add the meat, little by little, and fry for 10 minutes, or until the liquid dries up. Add the tomato and sauté for 3 minutes, or until it begins to undo.
  2. Add the tomato pulp, water, Seasoning SAZÓN and salt, and let it cook over low heat, with the pan half-covered, for 5 minutes, or until slightly thickened. Remove from fire.
  3. In a large rectangular refractory (23 x 35 cm), make layers with the sauce, pasta, ham and mozzarella, alternating and repeating until you finish with the sauce and the mozzarella.
  4. Cover with aluminum foil and place in a medium oven (180 degrees), preheated, for 20 minutes, or until the dough is soft. Remove the aluminum foil and return to the oven for another 5 minutes, or until the cheese is browned.
  5. Remove from the oven and serve immediately.
